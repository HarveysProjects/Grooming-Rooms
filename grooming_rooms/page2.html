About Us

Domestic Cleaning Services in Hertfordshire & Essex

We're on a mission to make homes in Herts & Essex sparkling clean !

We understand that when you're looking for domestic cleaners you want someone who makes sure that your own high standards are met. We offer a wide range of cleaning services designed for every sized home and budget.

At Grooming Rooms, we pride ourselves on the fact that:


  • You always have a cleaning team you can trust and rely on
  • You always have a fully insured cleaning team to clean your home (we provide a copy of our insurance document for your peace of mind)
  • All of our cleaners are friendly, flexible and reliable
  • We provide a range of cleaning services to suit all budgets and size of home
  • We provide a secure key holder service
  • We use non-toxic, high performance Method Cleaning Products, eCloths, eCover Eco Products and Dyson vacuum cleaners
  • We offer a fantastic oven cleaning service
  • Our first clean is the same as every clean
  • You will return home to a thoroughly clean, fresh smelling house - perfect!

Our service:


You get to choose which day our cleaners come to your home, Monday to Sunday. Generally speaking, you will always have the same dedicated team of two for each clean so that our staff have the opportunity to develop a good relationship with you and the geography of your home.

Payment is operated on either a weekly or monthly account and can be made direct to the company by one of the following methods - cash, cheque, standing order or bank transfer.

Special discounts are available for estate agents and landlords where more than one property is to be cleaned.
